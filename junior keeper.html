JUNIOR KEEPER

Junior Keepers is a great program for those future marine biologists! Come experience the aquarium from an employee’s point of view. Learn how to provide animal care, including: training, enrichment, and feeding to a variety of animals. You’ll go behind the scenes, meet live animals, and talk with our care staff. Junior Keepers are required to wear sneakers. Space is limited.

To book your spot for Junior Keepers, please select the children in your party below then select the date on the calendar you would like to visit. Available dates are shown in green on the calendar. General admission for the day included. Payment is required at booking. No Refunds. Participants who need to reschedule may do so by contacting education@jenkinsons.com.
